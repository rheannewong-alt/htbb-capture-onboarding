What happens next

Your next steps

📬
The Lead will be in touch
Now that you've completed the course, the Capture Team Lead will reach out to organise your shadow Sunday and get you added to Planning Center.
👁️
Your shadow Sunday
You'll be paired with an experienced team member for at least one Sunday to observe, shoot alongside, and ask questions in real time. No pressure — this is purely a learning experience.
📅
Added to Planning Center
Once you and the Lead feel ready, you'll be rostered onto Planning Center and start serving. Remember to block out any unavailable dates two months in advance so the Lead can plan accordingly.
📱
Join the team group chat
The Lead will add you to the Capture Team group chat — this is where reminders, shot list previews, schedule changes, and team updates are shared. Make sure your notifications are on.
🌱
Grow at your own pace
As you get more comfortable, there are opportunities to deepen your involvement — from learning Lightroom editing to eventually leading a Sunday shoot. Let the Lead know where you'd like to grow and they'll support you.

We're genuinely so glad you're here. Every photo you take is a small act of service — and over time, those moments add up to something beautiful. We can't wait to serve with you.

— The HTBB Capture Team
