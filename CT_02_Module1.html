Module 01

Who We Are &
Our Vision

Before you pick up a camera, it helps to know who HTBB is, why we exist, and where the Capture Team fits into that story.

Holy Trinity Bukit Bintang

HTBB — Holy Trinity Bukit Bintang — is a vibrant church in the heart of Kuala Lumpur, passionate about helping people encounter Jesus, grow in faith, and make a difference in the world.

We are part of the HTB Network and the Anglican Diocese of West Malaysia, committed to creating spaces where people from all walks of life feel welcomed, valued, and empowered to discover and operate in their purpose — all to make a difference in society and the world.

Our vision

To play our part in the evangelisation of the nation, the revitalisation of the church, and the transformation of society.

The HTB Network

HTB began in London and has grown into a global movement of churches sharing a common vision and culture. Being part of this network means we carry the same heart — reaching the lost, caring for the broken, and building community that transforms lives.

As the Capture Team, you are part of communicating that vision visually. The photos and content you create don't just document Sundays — they tell the story of what God is doing at HTBB to our city and beyond.

Why this matters

Many people will encounter HTBB for the first time through a photo on Instagram or a graphic in a Story. Your lens is often someone's first impression of our church. That's a meaningful responsibility — and a meaningful gift.

The Capture Team

The Capture Team is part of HTBB's broader Creative team. We exist to document the life of the church — worship, community, teaching moments, and the everyday in-between — through photography.

As a volunteer on this team, you are not just a photographer. You are a storyteller, a servant, and a steward of the moments that happen in this space every Sunday.

1
Document the Sunday experience
Worship, teaching, community moments, altar call, and everything in between.
2
Create content for social media
Your photos feed HTBB's Instagram, website, and communication channels — reaching people far beyond the Sunday room.
3
Preserve the memory of our church
Years from now, these photos will be the record of what God did in this season at HTBB.
4
Visual storytelling & digital pastoring
The HTBB Capture Team exists to tell stories and digitally pastor people through visual storytelling. More than just taking photos, the team captures moments of worship, community, and encounter in a way that helps people feel seen, connected, and invited into church life — both in-person and online.
